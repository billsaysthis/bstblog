I was wondering why Robert Scoble, usually fairly prolific with the posting, hadn't been seen in my aggregator for awhile so I went over to the site and saw the comments on his post supporting Molly Holzschlag. Geez, people get rough but some of these guys were going like they were sticking pins in a Scoble voodoo doll for pleasure. Some of the time I feel, though much more mildly, that Rob goes a bit too far into cheerleading mode but damn. Rob, we love you anyway, turn off comments if you must, but get back on the horse!
