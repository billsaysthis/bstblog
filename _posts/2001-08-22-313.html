Privacy? We give it away

Lots of folks argue that companies are stalking our right to privacy. But, as Evan Gahr writes in the Washington Post, too often for many people's tastes we give away way too much information in the office, on a train, and definitely on TV. Civilized people used to be discreet, now they are simplay discrete.
