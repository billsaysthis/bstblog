Publishing Science Fiction

I've been reading Analog more or less continuously since the late '60s and for most of that time Stan Schmidt has been the editor. Science Fiction Weekly, my favorite online SF magazine, has new author Michael Burstein interview Schmidt in the current issue and he has a few interesting things to say about the state of print SF magazine publishing.
