Over the weekend: Aquarium of the Pacific

Spent the weekend in LA at the New Otani in downtown LA; fortunately we missed all the Lakers/Kings traffic. High point was definitely a visit to the Aquarium of the Pacific in Long Beach. Beautiful setting, beautiful building, and great exhibits, especially the Jellies: Phantoms of the Deep exhibit.
