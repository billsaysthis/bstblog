Ordinary pet peeves are like:

Someone cutting you off or tailgating on the roads

Homicide bombers

Jerry Springer

But I have a serious one: putting a new roll of toilet paper out the wrong way. The hanging paper needs to be on the side away from the toilet seat but many people get this wrong. Why? Is it that hard to figure out? Come on people, let's get it right!
