After waiting for four months, I finally got to see the centennial Ansel Adams at 100 exhibit. Featuring over 100 wonderful photographs from perhaps the greatest naturalist--I'm happy to say I have three Adams prints on my living room wall--but get there fast as the party ends January 13. Also recommended: Matisse and Beyond and some cool new pieces like this Magritte.
