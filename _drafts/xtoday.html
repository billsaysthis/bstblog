Version 1

When you wake up in the morning
Do you take an easy breath or
Does your chest tighten
Fluttering rapidly like hummingbird wings?

When I look out the window I see the same
View as my Sweet One but somehow instead
We don't see the same thing, at least
What we see doesn't have the same impact on our souls.

When we drive to work I am thinking about
All that can go wrong that day and she jokes
About her co-workers or a show from last night and
I think she is the one of us afraid of everything.

When does the terror that keeps me up at night and
Invades my waking hours more and more often
Distracting me from most of the small bits of
Joy and happiness that I know can be had?
